What Is This?

TSpin Roulette generates a Tetris pattern or method randomly from the selected list.
It is intended as a companion learning tool or a training game.

How Do I Play?

Pick your favorite version of Tetris and start a new game.
At the same time, on TSpin Roulette, press 'r' on your keyboard or click the 'Restart' and a random pattern will appear.
Try to create that pattern in the Tetris game.
Once you successfully complete the pattern, press the spacebar or click the 'Random Pattern' button.
A new pattern will appear and your score will increment.
See how many patterns you can create before the Tetris game ends.
Note this is easiest if you play Tetris on a console and have TSpin Roulette open on a computer.
You can also click the name of a pattern to view it.

What If I Don't Want Certain Patterns?

Uncheck the box next to the pattern name and it won't be generated by TSpin Roulette.

Can I Use This on My Stream?

Yes.
